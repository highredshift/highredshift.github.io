Publications

Among other papers, our group leads two series
  • the Strong Lens Search Series,
  • the DESI Strong Lens Foundry Series

DESI Strong Lens Search Series

A multi-paper effort to discover strong gravitational lenses in DESI imaging and spectroscopy, using multiple ML and "classifical" techniques. For more details, see the Lens Search section.

DESI Strong Lens Foundry Series

Our group leads the DESI Strong Lens Foundry Series, a multi-paper effort to confirm and model strong gravitational lenses in DESI imaging and spectroscopy, with additional observatoins from Keck, VLT MUSE, VLT Xshooter, and Gemini. For more details, see the Lens Modeling section.
