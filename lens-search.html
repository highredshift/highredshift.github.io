Lens Search

We search for lenses in DESI imaging and spectroscopic data, using ML/AI and “classical” techniques.

  1. Deep-learning searches in DESI imaging using ResNet and EfficientNet; ~4,000 new lenses across four search papers:
    • Huang et al. 2020
    • Huang et al. 2021
    • Storfer et al. 2024
    • Inchausti et al. 2025
  2. Autocorrelation
    • Dawes et al. 2022 : 434 lensed quasar candidates found in DESI imaging quasar catalog.
  3. Difference Imaging
    • Sheu et al. 2023 : Restroscopic search of lenses supernovae in DESI imaging quasar catalog.
    • Sheu et al. 2024 : Lensed quasars found via a time-variabilty search in DESI imaging quasar catalog.
